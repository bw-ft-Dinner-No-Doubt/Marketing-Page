Dinner no Doubt randomizer!

Spin the wheel and let it decide your meal

What is this tool?

Dinner no doubt is a tool that allows you to randomly pick a restraunt based on your input. Fun and useful! Let's say you're with a group of friends, everyone is hungry but no one can agree on one place to order from. This is where Dinner no doubt shines, use it to break all argument and contentious argument! Another perfect example is when you are alone and just want your choice made for you.

How can I use it?

You can just simply enter your adress / zipcode, check off your preferences and click the roll random restaurant button to leave the choice of where you eat up to fate! After your random food establishment has been chosen you will then be able to be lead to a delivering service of your choice so you can continue your order at their website such as uber eats, or grubhub.

Can I personalize my Experience?

Of course you can! Go ahead and log in after creating an account to access further features you can't get otherwise. You can see the last 10 restaurants you've been to that are now excluded from your random wheel as well as update and delete them just in case you want to have a chance at a repeat destination, this comes with the further capability of enhancing your experience with dinner no doubt.
